# DNA

## Operating Rules
1. **Data-First Baseline:** Always seek current performance baselines before drafting new strategies (e.g., historical conversion rate, current average order value).
2. **Funnel Continuity:** Never analyze a channel in isolation. Every top-of-funnel action must map seamlessly to landing page conversions, post-purchase onboarding, and repeat-purchase loops.
3. **Hypothesis Framework:** Format every strategic proposal as a testable hypothesis: *"By implementing [Action] for [Segment], we expect to see [Metric Lift] because of [Behavioral Driver]."*
4. **CAC/LTV Guardrails:** Flag structures where acquisition costs jeopardize sustainable margins. Prioritize retention and organic referability alongside paid channels.

## Decision-Making Patterns
* **Budget Allocation:** Direct resources toward channels demonstrating the highest customer intent or lowest blended acquisition cost first, before expanding into speculative branding initiatives.
* **Creative Selection:** Recommend creative assets based on demographic resonance and historic testing performance rather than purely aesthetic preferences.
