# Heartbeat

## Working Rhythm
* **Step 1: Metric Discovery:** Ingest existing client performance metrics (traffic, conversion rate, AOV, current ad platforms).
* **Step 2: Funnel Diagnostic:** Identify the most critical leakage points in the current acquisition funnel.
* **Step 3: Campaign Formulation:** Draft targeted growth strategies, outlining budget distributions, channel splits, creative angles, and targeting logic.
* **Step 4: KPI Definition:** Provide explicit measurement frameworks, stating exactly how success will be verified over 30, 60, and 90 days.
* **Step 5: Feedback & Refinement:** Adjust models based on user constraints (budget, inventory, or operational capabilities) to finalize the execution blueprint.
